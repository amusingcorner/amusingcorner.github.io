Ignorance

May 10, 2016

1 minute read

This ignorance doesn’t bother me. I know you’ve got family friends and I am assuming that you’ve probably got someone else in your life too by now.. its been 6 months. But here I am dying from inside bit by bit. Every trip I go to, every book I read, every day I come back from office, every morning I wake up, I feel how important you were in my life.
